Privacy policy

Forget Me Not stores the email or phone number you provide and a securely hashed password after you finish signing up. When you press Create account, a verification code is generated (intended to be sent by email or SMS when those services are connected). Your account is only created after you enter that code.

This demo site keeps account data in a local file on the server (data/auth-store.json). Do not use it for real personal data until you replace storage with a production-grade database and add proper policies, data retention, and legal review.

Payments are handled by Stripe according to their terms when you complete checkout.
